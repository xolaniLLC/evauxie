Facilities and Capacity

The hotel can accommodate up to 135 guests in a banquet-style reception, or 150 guests cocktail-style. Couples can modify the multi-level banquet hall to reflect the theme of their special day, featuring high ceilings, mirrored walls, and plenty of natural light. The banquet hall contains two sets of elegant French doors leading onto a private terrace. Additionally, there is a grand mahogany bar and dance floor to keep your guests entertained for the night.

Services Offered

La Tourelle Hotel and Spa offers couples a range of services to help make their wedding vision a reality. They can provide event-planning assistance in the lead up to your celebrations and will allocate a wedding concierge at your serviceof the day. Their friendly staff will take care of setup and cleanup to help ensure that you can enjoy every special moment. They can also manage sound and lighting, as well as catering and bar services, to make organizing all the different your wedding easier.
